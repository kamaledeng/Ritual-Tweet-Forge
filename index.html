What this app is for

Ritual Agent Arena is a community playground for testing Ritual-native app ideas. Builders can compare agent strategies, share the result, then anchor the battle on Ritual Testnet with a small gas transaction.

Wallet is optional for simulation. Connect to attach your address to battle records.
